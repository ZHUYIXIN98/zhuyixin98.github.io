Impact of Disruption Risk at Different Supplier Tiers (Previous-used title: Supply Disruption in Multi-Tier Supply Chains: Competition and Network Configuration)
with Sean Zhou, Hongfan(Kevin) Chen
Under Review

We consider a three-tier supply chain, where a tier-0 firm (OEM) can source from two potential tier-1 suppliers, and each tier-1 supplier can source from two potential tier-2 suppliers. Either a tier-1 or a tier-2 supplier is susceptible to disruption risk. We study the impacts of disruption risk at different supplier tiers on the performance of centralized and decentralized supply chains. We further characterize supplier selection strategies of the OEM and tier-1 suppliers in the presence of fixed sourcing costs...
